Early Life / Life Before Presidency

Ronald Reagan was born in in Illinois on February 6th 1911. His father was a man named John Reagan (born: 1883 died: 1941) and his mother was a lady named Nelle Reagan (born: 1883 died: 1962). He also had a brother named Neil Reagan (born: 1908 died: 1996). Ronald Reagan went up to the education level of College. Ronald Reagan attended Eureka College and Graduated in 1932. At College he was involved in Theater and after College he went to Chicago to look for a job in broadcasting.
