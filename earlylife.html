Early Life / Life Before Presidency

Ronald Reagan was born in in Illinois on February 6th 1911. His father was a man named John Reagan (born: 1883 died: 1941) and his mother was a lady named Nelle Reagan (born: 1883 died: 1962). He also had a brother named Neil Reagan (born: 1908 died: 1996). Ronald Reagan went up to the education level of College. Ronald Reagan attended Eureka College and Graduated in 1932. At College he was involved in Theater and after College he went to Chicago to look for a job in broadcasting.

Ronald Reagan first movie with Hollywood was a movie called “Love Is on the Air”. Ronald Reagan made over 20 films. Ronald Reagan met his first wife Jane Wyman on the movie “Brother Rat”, they were married from January 26th, 1940 and divorced on June 28th, 1949 having one daughter and one adopted son together. While he was President of the Screen Actors Guild he met his second wife Nancy Davis (the future Nancy Reagan), and they married on March 4th 1952 and were married until Ronald Reagan death in 2004.

Ronald Reagan was elected Governor of California on August 9th, 1966 Ronald Reagan served two terms as the Governor of California (8 years as Governor of California)
